About Yael Goldmann

Yael Goldmann is a writer, journalist, and executive/life coach. Her life and work are shaped by a broad worldview, the result of having lived in different countries and experienced diverse cultures that enrich her perspective.

Author of novels that blend romance, suspense, and emotional depth, Yael specializes in portraying strong female characters who face intimate dilemmas and complex realities. Her stories, told with a warm and visual style, move between contemporary settings and historical contexts, addressing themes such as resilience, identity, politics, and human relationships.

Her literary career includes titles such as Identidades Ocultas, En el Nombre de Ella, Cuando Todo Calla, Destinos Entrela­zados, Encuentros Casuales, Jaque a la Reina, and Sobrevivir a la Vida, available on Amazon, Google Play, and other international platforms.

Beyond her work as a writer, Yael supports individuals and organizations in processes of personal and professional transformation, integrating effective communication, empathy, and strategic vision into her approach.

With a distinctive voice and a commitment to telling stories that move, entertain, and inspire reflection, Yael Goldmann continues to build a bridge between personal experience and fiction, inviting readers to look beyond the obvious and explore the emotions that define us.
