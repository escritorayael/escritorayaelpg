About Yael Goldmann

Yael Goldmann was born in Argentina and currently resides in Caracas, Venezuela. She began her career as a journalist, ventured into airplane marketing, took on roles as a financial advisor, and is now recognized as an executive and life coach. She maintains constant contact with her numerous followers through her Instagram account Coach_Yael_Goldmann.

When she's not writing, Yael enjoys traveling, discovering new cultures, and finding inspiration in the everyday moments that make life extraordinary.
